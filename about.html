About BlocHaus

Decentralizing Real Estate, One Bloc at a Time. Learn about our mission to make property ownership globally accessible through blockchain technology.

Platform Name: BlocHaus

Token Symbol: $BHS (Solana SPL Token)

Overview

BlocHaus is a decentralized real estate platform where users can buy, rent, sell, or invest in real estate using blockchain and cryptocurrency.

We're using the power of Web3, Solana, and tokenization to make property transactions faster, safer, and borderless — while still giving users access to real consultants for every deal.

Our Vision

"Decentralizing Real Estate, One Bloc at a Time."

To make property ownership and investing globally accessible — whether you're renting a short-term apartment or co-investing in luxury real estate, BlocHaus lets you do it with your wallet and our $BHS token.

Core Features

Buy, Rent, Sell, or Invest in Properties
  • • Users can explore verified properties, connect their wallet, and transact using $BHS.
  • • Sellers and property owners can list their homes or apartments for sale or lease.
Token-Powered Transactions
  • • All payments are done in $BHS — our native Solana token.
  • • Future features include staking, rewards, and governance access.
Real-Time Consultant Chat
  • • Every transaction is backed by a dedicated BlocHaus consultant.
  • • Users can chat via our integrated support system before, during, or after a deal.
Blockchain Escrow & Transparency
  • • Funds are held in Solana smart contract escrow until conditions are met.
  • • Buyers, sellers, and renters get transparent transaction records on-chain.
